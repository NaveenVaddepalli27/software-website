WELCOME TO CONZURA SOFT SOLUTIONS

Conzura Soft Solutions. was established in 2005 by a software engineer and very quickly proved itself as a top-notch engineering firm solving problems for Department of Defense customers.

We are a custom software development company. We provide expertise for the entire life-cycle project support, from gathering and defining requirements to running projects through to successful completion.

We specialize in Big Data and Cloud Solutions, Geographic Information Systems and Mapping, Information Retrieval and Search Engines.

Conzura Soft Solutions. is proud to be a woman owned small business serving the intelligence community.
